Frequently Asked Questions

Simply browse our collection, select the product you love, choose your size, and click "Order on WhatsApp". This will open a WhatsApp chat with your order details pre-filled. Our team will confirm availability and share payment details.
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS), and cash on delivery for select pin codes. Prepaid orders above ₹999 qualify for free shipping.
We offer exchanges within 7 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Returns are accepted for manufacturing defects. Please contact us on WhatsApp with photos for a quick resolution.
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days across India. Metro cities typically receive orders within 3–5 days. You'll receive a tracking link once your order is shipped.
Currently, we ship within India only. International shipping is coming soon! Follow us on Instagram for updates.
Each product page mentions available sizes. If you need help with measurements, send us a WhatsApp message and we'll guide you to the perfect fit. We're happy to share exact measurements for any product.
