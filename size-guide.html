Find Your Perfect Fit

All measurements below are body measurements, not garment measurements. Use a soft measuring tape for accurate results.

Step 1

How to Measure Yourself

Stand naturally and keep the tape snug but not tight. Measure over light inner-wear for best results.

📏

Bust / Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

✂️

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually just above your belly button.

🧵

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.

📐

Height

Stand straight against a wall, heels together. Measure from top of head to floor.

💡 Pro tip: If you are between two sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed, comfortable fit. All our garments carry approximately 1 inch of ease margin.

Fit Tips

A few things to keep in mind before you order

  • ✓ Size up at the border. If your measurements fall between two sizes, always choose the larger size for a comfortable, breathable fit.
  • ✓ 1 inch ease is built in. All garments include approximately 1 inch of ease on top of the stated body measurement — so the garment is never skintight.
  • ✓ Prioritise bust for tops. For kurtis and tops, match your bust measurement first. The waist and hem can always be slightly adjusted by a tailor.
  • ✓ Cotton garments may shrink slightly. We recommend washing in cold water on a gentle cycle to preserve the fabric and colour.
  • ✓ Hand-block prints vary slightly. Because our prints are hand-made by artisans, minor motif placement variations are a mark of authenticity, not a defect.

Care Quick Reference

Keep your Dhalam pieces looking fresh and beautiful

🧼

Hand Wash

Wash in cold water with a mild detergent. Avoid scrubbing.

🎨

Colour Care

Wash dark colours separately for the first 2–3 washes to prevent bleeding.

☀️

Dry in Shade

Avoid direct sunlight. Dry flat or hang in shade to retain shape and colour.

🔥

Iron on Low

Use medium heat. For block-printed fabrics, iron on reverse side.
